Study Sets

Study sets are a way of managing groups of features. The study sets can be accessed by opening the StudySet window from the Window menu.


What can I do with my study sets?
Right-clicking any study set will bring up the available options to analyze and view the details that study set.
Once a detail view is opened, the view can be updated by selecting other study sets.

Creating a study set:
Study sets can be created in the Parallel Sets view. A set can be created in the Parallel Sets view by right-clicking a ribbon.
